Mind = Blown.

Finished a two day workshop; CreativeJS for non–coders by Seb Lee–Delisle. Well, I’d reckon some basic knowledge of Javascript is advisable but Seb explains things pretty well with his visual aids.

The workshop is an introduction to HTML5 Canvas; learning the basics of creating shapes, colours, animations, particles, faking physics and such (with some cheating). We created a single–player Pong game by midday of the second day and a particle generator by the end of the workshop! Seb broke down the problems into tiny chunks and gave us small exercises of solving them; making the problem a lot less intimidating.

Good stuff!
